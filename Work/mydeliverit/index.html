About

We are a last mile logistics network that strives to simplify deliveries for E-commerce, Restaurants, FMCG, Pharma and online & offline retailers. We provide an on-demand service and aim to disrupt logistics by using tech to provide faster, efficient, low-cost deliveries. We are transforming door-to-door logistics with our best-in-class Service Operation Centre.
