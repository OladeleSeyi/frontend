About Us

“Deliver-IT” is a trendsetting on-demand last mile logistics network offering tech-enabled one stop delivery solutions for E-commerce, Restaurants, FMCG, Pharma and online & offline retailers. Our collaborative model is a combination of on demand and on the way models and is set to disrupt the logistics industry by enabling faster, more efficient, low-cost delivery for businesses whether big corporations or SMEs We are on a mission to transform how door-to-door delivery works by leveraging on tech-driven solutions; customized delivery procedures and a best-in-class service operation Centre, all in a bid to create a faster and more cost-efficient solution for you and your businesses.

Deliver-IT brings into play an elastic service model with the ability to quickly and seamlessly scale operations capacity to changing demands.

How We Work

Our simple and innate flow makes sending and receiving packages fast and convenient.

1. Schedule a pickup or delivery Place your orders via our callcenter, Whatsapp / email providing pickup point, dropoff point, receiver's info and parcel details.

2. Pick Up
Nearest Deliver-it Partner receives delivery request and arrives at pick up location in shortest possible time. We will take care of delivering your orders to their receivers and collecting cash on delivery if needed

3. Delivery
Deliver-it Partner reaches Drop Off location, verify Receiver’s details and handover the Package. We guarantee the best delivery experience for you and your customers
